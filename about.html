Our Story

The House of Blue Diamond Co.

A London jeweller built on a simple belief — that fine jewellery should be as personal as the moments it marks.

Who We Are

Craft, Conscience & Care

Blue Diamond Co. is a London name for diamonds, fine jewellery and luxury watches — plus a bespoke design service that turns your ideas into one-off pieces.

From our base in Clerkenwell, steps from the historic heart of London's jewellery trade, we bring together the finest stones and the most accomplished craftspeople. Every piece we offer — whether from our collection or made to commission — is chosen and finished with the same exacting standard.

We believe luxury is not loud. It is found in the cut of a stone, the weight of a band, and the quiet confidence of something made beautifully and meant to last.
