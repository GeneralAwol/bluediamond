Bespoke Studio

Built Around You

The best pieces are the ones made for you. Our designers and setters turn your idea into something you'll actually want to wear — no templates, no copies.

Begin Your Commission

The Bespoke Promise

One of One

Whether it's an engagement ring, a bespoke pendant, or a redesign of something you already own, every project starts with a conversation and ends with something that's genuinely one of a kind.

We walk you through stone choice, metal and setting, and share detailed 3D renders and a wax model before a single gem is set — plus a clear quote up front, so there are no surprises.

The Journey

How a Commission Comes to Life

Consultation

We meet to understand your vision, occasion, budget and timeline — in person or virtually.

Design

Our designers create sketches and 3D renders, refining every proportion until it feels right.

Craft

Master goldsmiths hand-build the piece and set each stone with meticulous care.

Reveal

Your finished piece is presented, fully certified and ready to wear — with aftercare on us.
